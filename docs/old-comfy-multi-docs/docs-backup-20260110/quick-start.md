## Step 3: Customize Your Prompt (2 minutes)

Find the blue box labeled **"CLIP Text Encode (Prompt)"**:

1. Click anywhere inside the blue box
2. Find the text field (it probably says something like "a beautiful landscape")
3. **Change it to YOUR idea!** For example:
   - `"a magical forest at sunset, glowing mushrooms, fantasy art"`
   - `"a cute robot reading a book, studio lighting, detailed"`
   - `"cyberpunk city at night, neon lights, cinematic"`

**Pro tip:** Be specific! The more details you add, the better the result.

**Q: Why is my job taking so long?**
A: Everyone shares one GPU. If there are 10 people ahead of you, you'll wait for ~10 jobs to finish. Be patient! â±ï¸

**Q: Where did my image go?**

**Q: Can I start over?**

**Q: What if something breaks?**
